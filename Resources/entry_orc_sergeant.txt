Orc Sergeant
Summary:*Orc Sergeants are Orcish fighters who are more powerful and well-equipped than normal Orcs. Orc Sergeants can be distinguished from their lesser brethren by their double-bladed axes, boots, and heavier armor. They can be hit by weapons of any material.
Advice:*Melee attacks. The Orcish language skill can sometimes render an enemy non-hostile.
