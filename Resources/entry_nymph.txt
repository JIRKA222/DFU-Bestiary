Nymph
Summary:*Nymphs are fae creatures of unearthly beauty that attack with mystical energy. They can be hit by weapons of elven quality or higher.
Advice:*Nymphs can be easily dispatched with a melee attack. The Nymph language skill can sometimes render an enemy non-hostile.
Abilities:*Nymphs drain 2 points of fatigue for every 1 point of health damage
 *Resistance to Paralysis and Poison
Spells:*None
