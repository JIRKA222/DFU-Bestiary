Giant Bat
Summary:*The giant bat is a flying animal commonly encountered at low levels, but not so often at higher levels.
Advice:*Melee attacks can quickly dispatch these beasts, though attacking with arrows or spells will greatly reduce your odds of catching a disease.
Material:*No special material is required
Language:*None
Abilities:*There is a 5% chance per hit of catching a disease from a giant bat.
Spells:*None
