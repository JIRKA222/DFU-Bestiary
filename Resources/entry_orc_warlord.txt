Orc Warlord
Summary:*Orc Warlords are elite warriors, by far the strongest and most dangerous members of the Orcish hordes. They appear similar to Orc Sergeants, with the addition of a tassel on their helms, capes, and gold bracers. They can be hit by weapons of any material.
Advice:*Use ranged attacks at lower levels, melee attacks at higher levels. The Orcish language skill can sometimes render an enemy non-hostile.
Abilities:*None
Spells:*None
