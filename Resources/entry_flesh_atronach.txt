Flesh Atronach
Summary:*Flesh Atronachs are golems made of stitched-together cadavers and possess a poison-based damage aura.
Advice:*Melee attacks are recommended. Lower level characters who wish to avoid melee may use electricity-based spells.
Material:*No special material is required
Language:*None
Abilities:*Healed by poison-based spells
 *Immunity to Paralysis, Poison and Disease
 *Regenerates Health in Darkness
 *Adrenaline Rush
 *Bonus to Hit Humanoids
 *Takes Damage from Holy Places
Spells:*None
