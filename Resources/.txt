Centaur
Summary:*Centaurs are half-man, half-horse monsters wielding spears or other weapons. They can be hit by weapons of any material.
Advice:*As centaurs are resistant to magic, melee attacks work best. The Centaurian language skill can sometimes render an enemy non-hostile.
