Summary - Undead
Overview:*Undead creatures consist of spirits, reanimated skeletons and corpses, and vampires. They are a recurring menace in dungeons, and some can be very dangerous. Some types of undead are carriers of disease. Most undead are able to detect invisible foes. Ghosts, Skeletal Warriors, Wraiths and Zombies are also often found underwater.
