Fire Atronach
Summary:*Fire Atronachs are golems constructed of flames and possess a fire-based damage aura.
Advice:*Melee attacks are recommended. Lower level characters who wish to avoid melee may use cold-based spells.
Material:*No special material is requiered
Language:*None
Abilities:*Healed by fire-based spells
 *Immunity to Paralysis, Poison, Fire and Disease
 *Regenerates Health in Darkness
 *Adrenaline Rush
 *Bonus to Hit Humanoids
 *Low Tolerance to Frost
 *Takes Damage from Holy Places
Spells:*None
