Bestiary
Dragonling
Summary:*Dragonlings are winged reptiles that resemble dragons and attack by breathing fire. They can be hit by weapons of any material.
Advice:*Melee attacks are best. Beware its fiery breath, for it is not a spell. Dragonlings are heavily armored and can take a lot of hits. The Dragonish language skill can sometimes render an enemy non-hostile.
Abilities:*Resistance to Magic
 *Immunity to Paralysis, Poison, and Disease
 *Regenerates Health in Darkness
 *Adrenaline Rush
 *Bonus to Hit Humanoids
 *Low Tolerance to Frost
 *Takes Damage from Holy Places
Spells:*None
