Ice or Frost Daedra
Summary:*Frost Daedra are wreathed in the cold mists of Oblivion. Not only do their warhammers pack a devastating punch, but Frost Daedra can cast cold-based spells.
Advice:*Close in quickly and use melee attacks to deny the Frost Daedra an opportunity to cast its ranged spells. You may also wish to protect yourself with Spell Reflection, Spell Resistance, or Spell Absorption, or attack with fire-based offensive spells.
Material:*Mithril or higher is required
Language:*Daedric
Abilities:*Can detect camouflaged or invisible foes
Spells:*Ice Bolt
 *Ice Storm
