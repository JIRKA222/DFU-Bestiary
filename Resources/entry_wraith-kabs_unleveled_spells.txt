Wraith
Summary:*Like ghosts, wraiths are the spirits of the dead, but are much more powerful than their counterparts - both in melee and with magic.
Advice:*Wraiths can do massive melee damage so the use of ranged attacks is recommended, except at higher levels where they can be easily killed with melee attacks.
Material:*Silver or higher is required
Language:*None
Abilities:*Can detect camouflaged or invisible foes
 *Resistance to Magic
Spells:*Far Silence
 *Wraith Screech
