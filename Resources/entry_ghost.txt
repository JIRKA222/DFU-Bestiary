Ghost
Summary:*Ghosts are the angry spirits of the dead. Ghosts can be hit by weapons of silver quality or higher.
Advice:*At lower levels, ghosts are best attacked from a distance, or in melee while protected by Spell Reflection, Spell Resistance, or Spell Absorption. Higher level characters can easily destroy a ghost with melee attacks.
